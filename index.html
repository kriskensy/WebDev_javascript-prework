Welcome to the game Rock Paper Scissors

How to play?

  • Rock beats Scissors.
  • Scissors beats Paper.
  • Paper beats Rock.
  • If both players make the same move, the outcome is a draw.

Game Rules

  • Select Your Move: Click on the button for Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  • Computer's Move: The computer will make a random selection.
  • See the Result: The game will show your choice, the computer's choice, and the outcome of the round.
  • Winning the Game: The first player to reach 3 points wins the game.
  • Restarting the Game: To start over, click the "New Game" button.
  • Resetting Points: You can reset the score and start fresh anytime by clicking the "Reset Points" button.
